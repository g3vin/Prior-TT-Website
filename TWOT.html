Our Team Members

  • Mandy Molnar MOTR-CDP Owner/Therapist

    Mandy graduated with her Masters in Occupational Therapy from The University of North Dakota. She has 17 years of experience working as an Occupational Therapist and in Dementia Care. She has advanced education and is a Certified Dementia Practitioner. Mandy’s passion is finding what each of her client’s love, such as music, and incorporating them into her treatment. She loves to cook, hike, camp, and travel with her Husband Steve and Daughter Siri. Mandy also has 2 fur babies Tillie and Bailey!

  • Sherry Laidly COTA/L, CDP Owner/COTA

    I love my work! My day consists of helping people improve their safety, ease of daily life skills, and overall quality of life! I have treated all ages, diagnosis, conditions, but especially passionate about working with clients with Dementias/Alzheimer’s. It takes TLC, personalization, continuum of care, and good communication/education with client’s families and their caregivers. I have been blessed with serving clients for over 12 years as a Certified Occupational Therapy Assistant (COTA/L) and Certified Dementia Practitioner (CDP) . I reside in Lakewood, CO and live with my loving husband Jay and my rescue mutt Kaylee.

  • Linda Larkee PT, CDP

    Originally from Arkport, NY, then graduating from the University of Pittsburgh in 1986 with a B.S. in Physical Therapy, Linda started her career in South Carolina. She then landed in Colorado after a career opportunity for her husband in 1998. She has over 32 years of experience in multiple settings including hospitals, outpatient clinics, home health, adult day care centers, and nursing homes. Her true passion is working with adults, especially geriatrics. “Your goal is my goal. I appreciate and understand the importance of maximizing your functional level so you can live in the most non-restrictive environment successfully, and we will work hard towards your goals.” Linda loves outdoor activities including fishing, camping, hiking, hunting with her husband, gardening and baking rye bread. She has 2 children and loves being “Grammy” to 2 as well!
